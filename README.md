🚀 **Case Study: Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard**

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based businesses, directly impacting revenue and long-term growth. In this project, I analyzed customer data to identify the **key drivers behind churn and actionable strategies to improve retention**.

📊 **Problem Statement:**
The company was experiencing a high churn rate (~27%), leading to significant revenue loss. The goal was to uncover *why customers are leaving* and *how the business can reduce churn*.

🔍 **Key Insights:**
• Customers with **month-to-month contracts** show the highest churn rate, indicating low commitment
• **Electronic check payment users** have higher churn compared to other methods
• **Churn decreases as tenure increases**, meaning early-stage customers are at higher risk
• High-value customers contribute significantly to revenue, making their retention critical

💡 **Recommendations:**
• Encourage long-term contracts through discounts or incentives
• Improve onboarding experience for new customers
• Promote secure and reliable payment methods
• Focus on retaining high-value customers through personalized offers

📈 Built an interactive dashboard using **Power BI & DAX** to transform raw data into meaningful business insights and decision-making tools.

This project helped me understand how data analytics can drive **real business impact**, just like in consulting environments.
